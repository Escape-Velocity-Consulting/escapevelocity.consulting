Usage Rules

  • "Velocity" (or "V") always in terracotta; "Escape" (or "E") in light/dark depending on background
  • Use the horizontal wordmark as default; square for avatars, app icons, favicons; monogram only when space forces it
  • Transparent variants for overlays on imagery or colored backgrounds — ensure sufficient contrast
  • Do not recolor, rotate, or apply effects. Do not place the wordmark on low-contrast backgrounds.

Voice

Voice & Copy

Dos

  • Konkret vor abstrakt — lead with outcome
  • Active voice, short sentences
  • Numbers when possible ("6 Wochen", "17 Jahre")
  • "Sie" form (formal, not stiff)
  • Dry wit welcome; forced enthusiasm not

Don'ts

  • No "revolutionieren", "disruptiv", "game-changer"
  • No excessive exclamation marks
  • No "Wir freuen uns..." opener
  • No filler: "In diesem Zusammenhang..."
  • No AI-forward positioning as headline

Copy Patterns

  • Eyebrows: TRANSFORMATION & AUTOMATION — uppercase, 2–4 words
  • CTAs: imperative + arrow — Erstgespräch buchen →
  • Logo: always "Velocity" in terracotta
  • Tagline: "Digitalisierung & Prozessoptimierung" (DE)
